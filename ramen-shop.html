Ramen Restaurant

Description

A website that advertises an authentic Japanese ramen restaurant made with traditional techniques and fresh, quality ingredients

Color palette

Dominant color - white- 60%

Secondary color - grey - 30%

Accent color - brown red - 10%

Color mood

  • Silver grey with blue tins gives a cool and fresh vibe. It is a modern dynamic color.
  • Brown red is a wamn and earthly color. It symbolizes traditions. It is a good contrast for the cold and "trendy" grey tones.

Typography

This hand-drawn cursive display font that is bold enough to look great as a header text. Its curves and elegant lines give the website a vintage or romantic touch.

It is an easily legible modern geometric font. It has a clean and sleek aesthetic that gives the website an exquisite look and feel.

A serif font and a sans serif fonts are a harmonious combination. I used the font sporadically for secondary buttons to accentuate their contrast comparing to other text elements.

Webpage settings

Header

In the header image the bright circles accentuate dishes with their bright colors. It nicely communicates the main feature of the restaurant - all sorts of dishes with berries.

Content section

  • The section that comes right after the header showcases several dishes from different categories where the circles on imagery subtly repeats the circle as in the company berry logo pattern.
  • Next section encourages users to make an online reservation from the website. Once they choose the number of guests, they will be also shown availables time and dates.
  • The forth section is contact details with a CTA to call the restaurant. It is an easy way to get in touch and make an order or book a table.
