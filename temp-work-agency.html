Temporary Work Agency

Description

A website redesign of a large temporary work agency based in Germany. The company's main focus are technical specializations in such fields as engineering, electrics and logistics.

Brief Summary

  • Company: Temporary Work Agency
  • Region: Germany
  • Challenge: Webseite-Relaunch

User Groups

  • Main User Group: Job seekers - male, age 25 - 55
  • Secondary User Groups: Students, female job seekers, companies in search of personnel

Look and Feel

Dynamic patterns that look like building blocks or a ladder

Trustworthy and approachable

Friendly and modern

Color palette

Dominant color - pure white

Secondary color - dark brown

Accent colors - orange, yellow-green

Color choice

  • Wtite and dark brow are neutral colors that go well with bright colors.
  • Orange - brand logo color, used together with yellow-green as accent colors.

Color contrast: Design decisions were made with the contrast considerations (WCGA AA or AAA)

Sections

Landing page blocks are ordered according to what users might find most relevant on the platform starting with the most important blocks first.
